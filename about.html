About Saizor

Independent AI advice for organisations that want clarity — not a sales pitch.

Saizor is a Perth-based AI consultancy founded on a simple belief: every organisation deserves honest, independent advice about AI — without the jargon, without the sales pitch and without the conflict of interest.

Unlike most AI firms, we audit first and build second. We will never recommend an AI system until we have independently assessed whether you actually need it — and whether it will deliver real value for your business.

We have completed hundreds of AI maturity audits across Western Australia, working with businesses in mining services, logistics, construction, professional services and more. Our team combines deep AI expertise with real business experience. We speak the language of your boardroom, not just your IT department.

We are proud to be Perth-based — because local businesses deserve local experts who understand the WA market, its industries and its unique opportunities.
